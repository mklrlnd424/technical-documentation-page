Program Description

The contents of this website give a low level overview of the Jacked and Tan 2.0 liting program developed by strength and conditioning coach Cody Lefever. For a more comprehensive breakdown of the structure and progression of this program, please visit his website.

Weekly Schedule:4x Weekly training sessions. An upper/lower split with T2 and T3 back work done on either type of day, up to lifter preference. A fifth day is optional.


Day 1: Squat or deadlift variety

Day 2: Bench or overhead press variety

Day 3: Active recovery day

Day 4: Deadlift of squat variety

Day 5: Overhead press or bench variety
